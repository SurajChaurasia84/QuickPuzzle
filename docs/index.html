PRIVACY POLICY • Last Updated: June 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Top Puzzle. We value your trust and are fully committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy document outlines what information we process, how it is handled, and the principles we apply to maintain a clean, secure, and track-free gaming environment for our players.

2. Zero Data Collection Policy

Our philosophy is simple: we believe your data belongs to you. Therefore, Top Puzzle does not collect, capture, store, transmit, or share any personal identity information. Specifically:

  • We do not require user accounts, names, phone numbers, or email sign-ups to play the game.
  • We do not record your precise geolocation or search parameters.
  • We do not trace device IDs, IMEI numbers, or hardware specifications for advertising purposes.

There are no analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or tracking behaviors integrated inside the application binary.

3. Local Image Slicing & Processing

Top Puzzle allows players to select custom images to slice into puzzle grids. All calculations, cropping, image rendering, and coordinate offsets are processed strictly on-device within the local sandbox environment. The application does not upload your image data to remote servers for compilation or styling analysis.

4. Firebase Services & Security

We utilize standard Firebase Firestore connections to fetch puzzle options (e.g., dynamic preview image URLs uploaded by our admin dashboard). All read queries to Firebase are securely governed by database rules. No user metrics, performance diagnostics, or browsing histories are recorded through these connections.

5. Third-Party Links

The app contains direct external actions, such as the Share App feature (which copies a textual invite message) and Help & Support (which triggers standard native mail intents on your device). Opening links or invoking external mail applications is subject to the terms and privacy regulations of those respective third-party applications.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service does not collect any data from children or adults alike. Top Puzzle is completely safe for all audiences, including children under the age of 13. Since we do not request or gather personal information, we comply fully with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) regulations.
